FLIPKART PRICE ALERT SYSTEM

The Flipkart Price Alert System is a web application designed to help users track and manage the prices of products on Flipkart, one of India's leading e-commerce platforms. By setting personalized alerts, users can receive notifications when the price of their desired products drops or meets their preferred budget. This system enhances the shopping experience by allowing users to make informed purchasing decisions and potentially save money.
